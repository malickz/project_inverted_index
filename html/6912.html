IBC PRESIDENT TO ATTEND MANAGUA COFFEE MEETING

Brazilian Coffee Institute (IBC) president Jorio Dauster said he will attend a meeting in Managua this weekend. He told s by telephone from Brasilia that the meeting, involving Brazil, Colombia and Central American coffee producers, will be strictly to review the coffee market situation. "The meeting is set for Saturday but could also continue on Sunday," he said.
