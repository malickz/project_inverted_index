SPANISH UNEMPLOYMENT RISES IN FEBRUARY

The number of Spanish registered unemployed rose by 15,608 to 2.98 mln or 21.5 pct of the workforce in January in the sixth consecutive monthly increase, the Labour Ministry said. Registered unemployed totalled 2.81 mln or 21.1 pct of the workforce in February 1986.
