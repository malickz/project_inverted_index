HOKKAIDO TAKUSHOKU CONVERTIBLE EUROBOND COUPON CUT

The coupon on the 100 mln dlr, 15-year, convertible eurobond for Hokkaido Takushoku Bank Ltd has been set at 1-3/4 pct compared with the indicated two pct, joint-lead bookrunner Yamaichi International (Europe) Ltd said. The conversion price was set at 1,052 yen, while the foreign exchange rate was fixed at 146.20 yen per dollar.
