BECOR BCW TO ADJOURN DELAYED HOLDERS MEETING

Becor Western Inc said no business will be transacted at the shareholders meeting scheduled for 1000 CDT tomorrow and the meeting will be permanently adjourned. If a definitive agreement or transaction results from any of the offers now pending for the company's stock, Becor said, a date for a new shareholders meeting will be established.
