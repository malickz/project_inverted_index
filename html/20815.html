FEDERAL REALTY FRT TO BUY BACK SHARES

Federal Realty Investment Trust said its management has been authorized to purchase up to one mln of its common shares at current market prices in open market transactions. The company had 13.6 mln shares outstanding as of September 30. The company said it was making the purchases because its stock represented an excellent investment in light of current market conditions. Federal Realty will finance the buy-back through available cash funds.
