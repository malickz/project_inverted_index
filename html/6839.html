LOTUS LOTS CUTS PRICE ON 1-2-3 REPORT WRITER

Lotus Development Corp said it reduced the suggested retail price of its 1-2-3 Report Writer software package to 95 dlrs from 150 dlrs. The company said it also removed copy protection from the program, which is used to generate customized reports and forms from data base files. Lotus said the steps bring 1-2-3 Report Writer in line with other programs based on the 1-2-3 package.
