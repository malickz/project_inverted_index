PHOENIX STEEL RECEIVES OFFER FOR CLAYMONT MILL

Phoenix Steel Corp said a group of investors offered to buy its Clayton steel plate mill for eight mln dlrs and the assumption of a bond obligation. Phoenix did not disclose the indentity of the investors. Phoenix was forced to close the Clayton mill last month. The company said the offer represents a major step in restructuring the company.
