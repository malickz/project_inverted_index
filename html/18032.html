CRAY CYR GETS 3.6 MLN DLR COMPUTER ORDER

Cray Research Inc said Aerospatiale, a French aerospace company, ordered a Cray X-MP/14se computer system valued at about 3.6 mln dlrs. It said the leased system will be installed in the fourth quarter, pending export license approval.
