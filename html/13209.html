KENNER PARKER KPT CONCLUDES TWO SUITS

Kenner Parker Toys Inc's Parker Brothers said it obtained injunctions in two trademark infringement cases. One action was brought against Kits Games, which planned to market a game called Worldopoly, which was very similar to Kenner's Monopoly. The second case was brought against Citigames of America Inc which marketed a game called Hometown Monopoly.
