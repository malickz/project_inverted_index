BELGIAN PRIME MINISTER DEFENDS AUSTERITY PROGRAM

Belgian Prime Minister Wilfried Martens said government austerity programs adopted in the last six years had resolved some of the economy's urgent problems, but that unemployment remained a major obstacle. Martens told Belgian television the austerity measures had brought the country's balance of payments from deficit to surplus, helped reduce inflation and boosted economic growth.
