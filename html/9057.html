INTERLEUKIN-2 ILTO DETAILS WARRANT CONVERSION

Interleukin-2 Inc said the registration of warrants became effective March 20 and that 3.5 mln warrants will be convertible into 1.75 mln shares of the company's common stock, giving the company a maximum of 2.4 mln dlrs. The warrants must be exercised by June 22.
