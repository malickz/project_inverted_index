COLOROCS CLRX SIGNS AGREEMENTS WITH SHARP

Colorocs Corp said it signed a product supply agreement with Sharp Corp under which Sharp will be the exclusive manufacturer of Colorocs full color copiers. In addition, the company said it signed a licensing agreement under which Sharp will sell a version of the Colorocs copier under the Sharp name. The companies previously announced a joint copier development program.
