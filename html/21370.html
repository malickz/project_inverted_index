19-OCT-1987 10:49:52.59

U.S. ENERGY SECRETARY IN MOROCCO AFTER GULF TOUR

U.S. Energy Secretary John Herrington arrived in Rabat on Monday after a tour of the Gulf and the Middle East. Herrington, who is scheduled to meet King Hassan during his 24-hour official visit, said on arrival he would discuss regional and international issues with Moroccan leaders. Morocco is the last stop of a tour that has taken Herrington to Iraq, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia and Egypt.
