CHRYSLER C TO PUT FOUR PLANTS ON OVERTIME

Chrysler Corp's Chrysler Motors unit said it will put four of its U.S. assembly plants on overtime during the week of March 16. Three of the plants will also operate Saturday, March 14, beyond the normal five-day work week. Chrysler facilities at Newark, Del., Sterling Heights, Mich., Warren, Mich., and the Jefferson plant in Detroit will work overtime hours during the week. Newark, Sterling Heights and Jefferson will work Saturday.
