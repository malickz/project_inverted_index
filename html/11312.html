VLI CORP VLIC TO ENTER CONDOM MARKET

VLI Corp said it is in the final planning stages for the introduction of a line of condoms that it would introduce in the second half. It said it is in the process of selecting a supplier. In addition, the company said in the third quarter it will start marketing in the U.S. the Clearplan ovulation test kit, made by Unilever PLC UL.
