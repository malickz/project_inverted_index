MORGAN KEEGAN MOR SHARE OFFER UNDERWAY

Morgan Keegan Inc said an offering of one common of its common shares is underway at 15.25 dlrs per share through underwriters led by First Boston Inc FBC, A.G. Edwards and Sons Inc AGE and its own Morgan Keegan and Co Inc subsidiary. Underwriters have been granted an option to buy up to 150,000 more shares to cover overallotments.
