SJNB SJNB SAYS CHIEF EXECUTIVE RESIGNS

SJNB Financial Corp said Douglas McLendon has resigned as president and chief executive officer of the holding company and its San Jose National Bank subsidiary and as a director of its Tri-Valley National Bank subsidiary to pursue other interests. It said vice chairman William Pfeifle, 68, will act as interim president and chjief executive officer.
