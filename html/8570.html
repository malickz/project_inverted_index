GEODYNE GEOD TO OFFER PARTNERSHIP UNITS

Geodyne Resources Inc said it will begin selling immediately 300 mln dlrs worth of limited partnership interests in PaineWebber/Geodyne Energy Income Program II. The company said a unit will serve as general partner of the partnership, which will acquire oil and gas producing properties from third parties. PaineWebber PWJ will serve as the dealer-manager for the offering, it said.
