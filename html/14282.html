WEIS MARKETS WMK IN THREE FOR TWO STOCK SPLIT

Weis Markets Inc said its board declared a three-for-two stock split for holders of record May 1, 1987, with a distribution date of May 22, 1987. The company also said a regular quarterly dividend of 16 cts per share on the pre-split shares will be paid May 22 to shareholders of record May 1.
