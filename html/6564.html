NAT'L SAVINGS BANK NSBA HIRES NEW PRESIDENT

National Savings Bank of Albany said Thomas P. Bilbao will join the company as President and Chief Executive officer next month. He was fomerly a Senior Vice President for Goldome FSB. National Savings said Bilbao succeeds George O. Pfaff, who was elected chairman of the board and continues as chief executive officer.
