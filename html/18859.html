LEASEWAY TRANSPORTATION LTCCOMPLETES UNIT SALE

Leaseway Transportation Corp said it has completed the previously-announced sale of its Leaseway of Puerto Rico Inc subsidiary to Caguas Central Federal Savings Bank for undisclosed terms. The company said the sale satisfied a condition for its proposed acquisition by an investor group led by Citicorp CCI.
