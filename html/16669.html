GENERAL MOTORS GM GETS 97 MLN DLR CONTRACT

General Motors Corp is being awarded a 97 mln dlr Air Force contract for 176 turboprop engines in three separate designs, the Defense Department said. It said the contract, which combines purchases for the U.S. Navy, the U.S. Air Force Reserves, Japan and Norway under the Foreign Military Sales program, is expected to be completed in December 1987.
