SWEDEN ISSUES SEVEN-YEAR DOMESTIC BOND

Sweden's National Debt Office said it was issuing a four billion crown, seven-year domestic government bond with a coupon of 10.75 pct. It invited bids by April 6 and payment date for the loan is April 10. The issue, like all Swedish domestic paper, is closed to non-residents.
