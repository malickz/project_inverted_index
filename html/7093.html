TELECOM TELE SAYS SALE CLOSING DELAYED

Telecom Plus International Inc said the closing of the sale of its 65 pct interest in Tel Plus Communications Inc to Siemens AG has been delayed. The company said it will be continuing its talks with Siemens and based on current circumstances believes the transaction could close next week. Closing had been scheduled for March 16.
