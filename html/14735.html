FIRST TORONTO COMPLETES ISSUE

First Toronto Capital Corp said it completed an issue of a five mln dlrs convertible debenture to Arcalex B.V., a Dutch corporation that owns 54.3 pct of First Toronto's outstanding common shares. The debenture is convertible to one mln common shares of First Toronto for a period of five years.
