ALLSTAR INNS BEGINS PUBLIC OFFERING

Allstar Inns LP said it began a public offering of 5,832,037 depositary units priced at 12.50 dlrs a unit through lead underwriter Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc and co-managing underwriters Dean Witter Reynolds Inc and Bateman Eichler Hill Richards. The units represent limited partners' interest in the company. Proceeds will be used to repay debt.
