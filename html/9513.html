O'BRIEN ENERGY OBS IN CITICORP CCI LEASE

O'Brien Energy Systems Inc said it has signed a 12-year 12 mln dlr lease with Citicorp covering its wood gasification project in Quincy, Fla. O'Brien said it retains rights to the residual value of the project and may participate in the development of additional electric generating facilities as part of a second phase of the project, which started operating January One.
