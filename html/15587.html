FEDERATED DEPARTMENT STORES FDS MARCH SALES UP

Federated Department Stores Inc said sales for the five weeks ended April Four were up 4.9 pct to 934.9 mln dlrs from 891.6 mln dlrs a year before., The company said sales for the first two months of its fiscal year were up 6.8 pct to 1.65 billion dlrs from 1.55 billion dlrs a year before.
