CHRYSLER CSETS RECORD 1987 IMPORT TRUCK SALES

Chrysler Corp said its import operations closed out its 1987 model year with record sales of Dodge Ram-50 models of 76,913 trucks compared with the previous record 72,629 trucks sold in 1986. The Ram-50 is imported for Dodge from Mitsubishi Motors Corp.
