LSI LOGIC CANADA FILES FOR OFFERING

LSI Logic Corp of Canada Inc said it filed for a proposed public offering of common shares to be underwritten by McLeod Young Weir Ltd and Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Inc. The company did not give details of the proposed offering. LSI Logic is associated with, but not owned by, LSI Logic Corp LLSI.
