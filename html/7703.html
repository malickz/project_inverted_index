AMAX AMX FILES TO OFFER 15.8 MLN SHARES

AMAX Inc said it has filed for an offering of 15,757,350 common shares, including 757,300 to be sold by Mitsui and Co Ltd MITSY. AMAX said it will offer 12,607,350 shares in the U.S. through underwriters led by First Boston Inc FBC, along with American Express Co's AXP Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc subsidiary, Goldman, Sachs and Co and Merrill Lynch and Co Inc MER. Remaining shares will be sold outside the U.S. through underwriters led by First Boston's Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd affiliate, Shearson, Goldman Sachs and Merrill Lynch. AMAX said it will use its proceeds to reduce revolving credit debt and repurchase or repay from time to time other debt.
