U.K. MONEY MARKET GIVEN LATE HELP OF 210 MLN STG

The Bank of England said it gave the market late assistance of around 210 mln stg, bringing its total help on the day to some 353 mln stg. This compares with the Bank's estimate of the liquidity shortage of around 450 mln stg, raised from its early forecast of 400 mln stg.
