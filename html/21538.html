EQUITICORP HOLDING IN GUINNESS REACHES 59.93 PCT

Equiticorp Holdings Ltd EQUW.WE now owns or has received acceptances representing 59.93 pct of the issued ordinary share capital of Guinness Peat Group Plc GNSP.L, Equiticorp said in a statement. Equiticorp's offer for Guinness Peat became unconditional on October 3, when it had 50.6 pct, and closed on October 17.
