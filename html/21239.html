PERRY DRUG PDS DIRECTOR RESIGNS

Perry Drug Stores Inc said Donald Fox has resigned from the board of directors, effective immediately. Fox, a board director since 1971, said he was resigning because of new business interests, the company reported. Fox left Perry as president and chief operating officer in January and was recently appointed president of Beauty Brands Group, a distributor based in Southfield, Mich., the company said.
