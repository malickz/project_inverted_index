THERAGENICS THRX.O GETS CANCER PROCESS PATENT

Theragenics Corp said it will receive a U.S. patent on its TheraSeed localized radiation therapy for treatment of prostate cancer, effective June 23. It said it has obtained product liability insurance for Theraseed and expects commercial sales and distribution to start by July 15.
