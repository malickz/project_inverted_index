JEFFERIES JEFG MARKETS DIAMOND SHAMROCK DIA

Jefferies and Co Inc said it intends to make a market in the stock of Diamond Shamrock Corp, when, as and if distributed. Diamond Shamrock has offered to repurchase twenty miillion shares of its common stock at 17 dlrs per share until midnight, EST, March 9.
