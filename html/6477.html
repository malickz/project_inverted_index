CANAMAX TO ACQUIRE KREZMAR GOLD PROPERTY STAKE

Canamax Resources Inc said it agreed to acquire the 50 pct interest it does not already own in the Krezmar gold property, near Wawa, Ontario, by paying nine mln dlrs to Algoma Steel Corp Ltd and granting Algoma a four pct net smelter return royalty, which is payable after payback. The property's drill indicated reserves to a depth of 1,200 feet are estimated at over one mln tons averaging 0.25 ounces of gold a ton, Canamax said.
