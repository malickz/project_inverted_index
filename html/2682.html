FIELDCREST CANNON FLD TO SELL CONVERTIBLE DEBT

Fieldcrest Cannon Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 100 mln dlr issue of convertible subordinated debentures. Proceeds will be used to reduce outstanding bank debt under the company's revolving credit agreement, Fieldcrest Cannon said. The company named Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc as lead underwriter of the offering.
