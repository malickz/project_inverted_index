GERMAN NET CURRENCY RESERVES RISE

West German net currency reserves rose by 200 mln marks in the fourth week of March to 82.2 billion, following a rise of 300 mln marks in the previous week, the Bundesbank said. Non-currency reserves were unchanged at about 2.5 billion marks, bringing net monetary reserves to 84.7 billion.
