FINNAIR TO BUY TWO MCDONNELL DOUGLAS AIRCRAFTS

McDonnell Douglas Corp MD said Finnair, the national airline of Finland, will purchase two McDonnell Douglas MD-11 advanced long-range tri-jets with options for two more. Terms were not immediately disclosed. The first of the aircraft will be delivered in October 1990 and the second in May 1991.
