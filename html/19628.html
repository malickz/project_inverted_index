ALLIS-CHALMERS AH LISTING EXAMINED BY NYSE

The New York Stock Exhcange said it is reviewing the eligiblity for continued listing of the common stock and 5.875 dlrs cumulative convertible preferred stock, series C, of Allis-Chalmers Corp in view of its announcement that it has filed a voluntary petition under Chapter 11 of the Federal bankruptcy code.
