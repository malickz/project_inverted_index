GREAT LAKES GLK IN VENTURE WITH JOHNSON JNJ

Great Lakes Chemical Corp said it agreed to make an ingredient to be used by McNeil Specialty Products Co, a subsidiary of Johnson and Johnson, in the manufacture of sucralose, a high intensity sweetener. Sucralose is currently under review by the Food and Drug Administration. Great Lakes Chemical and McNeil Specialty will build the facilities needed for production, it said.
