BUSINESSMAN HAS ROYAL RESOURCES RRCO.O STAKE

James Stuckert, a Louisville, Ky., businessman, told the Securities and Exchange Commission he has acquired 380,000 shares of Royal Resources Corp, or 5.7 pct of the total outstanding common stock. Stuckert said he bought the stake for 600,000 dlrs solely as an investment.
