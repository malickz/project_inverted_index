3-MAR-1987 13:08:10.59

IMTEC IMTC GETS MERGER OFFER

Imtec Inc said some shareholders of Computer Identics Inc CIDN have proposed a merger of the two companies. The company said the shareholders had previously expressed dissatisfaction with Computer Identics' management and had informed Computer Identics that the present board no longer had the support of a majority of shares held. It said the shareholders had called for the resignation of all but one of Computer Identics' directors and suggested that a new board pursue merger talks with Imtec. But Imtec said no merger talks havew yet taken place.
