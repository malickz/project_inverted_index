HADSON CORP HADS.O COMPLETES ACQUISITION

Hadson Corp said it completed the acquisition of 85 pct of the Seaxe Energy Corp's SEAX.O common stock for 182,415 shares of Hadson's stock. Seaxe is engaged in international oil and gas exploration and development primarily in the Paris basin of France.
