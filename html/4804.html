NATIONAL ENTERTAINMENT NENT REVERSE SPLIT SET

National Entertainment Corp said shareholdersapproved a one-for-25 reverse stock split and a name change to Major Video Corp, both effective March 16. It said its new ticker symbol will be MAJV. The company also said it expects to add nine company-owned and franchised Major Video stores in the next 60 days. It now operates 64.
