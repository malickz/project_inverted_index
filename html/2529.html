ARGENTINE INFLATION ROSE 6.5 PCT IN FEBRUARY

Argentina's cost of living index grew 6.5 pct in January, down from last month's 7.6 pct, the National Statistics Institute said. It said consumer prices rose 98.9 pct over the last 12 months, against 81.9 pct inflation in the calendar year 1986.
