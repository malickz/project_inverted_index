24-APR-1987 10:48:51.00

FED SPOKESMAN SAYS NO NEWS CONFERENCE PLANNED

The Federal Reserve Board has no news conference or announcement planned for today, a Fed spokesman said, dispelling market rumors that a statement was pending. "No such event is scheduled or planned," said spokesman Bob Moore. Rumors of a pending Fed announcement or news conference circulated in financial markets, following a government report that consumer prices advanced by 0.4 pct in March, suggesting a rapid rise of inflation.
