TRACOR TRR GETS LOCKHEED LK CONTRACT

Tracor Inc said it has received a research contract worth over one mln dlrs from Lockheed Corp for the development of exploding foil initiated ordnance devices for the Exoatmospheric Reentry Interceptor Susbystem, or ERIS, Functional Technology Validation Interceptor Vehicle.
