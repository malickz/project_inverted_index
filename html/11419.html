W.R. GRACE GRA UNIT BEGINS PLANT EXPANSION

W.R. Grace and Co said its European Darex division has started constuction to double capacity at its fluid cracking catalyst plant in Worms, West Germany. The expansion is expected to cost approximately 20 mln dlrs, it said. The expansion is in response to demand for these catalysts in Europe and the Middle East. Completion of the project is scheduled for the middle of 1988, the company said.
