INTERNATIONAL LEASE FINANCE ILFC PICKS ENGINE

International Lease Finance Corp said it picked the CFM International CFM56-5 high bypass turbofan engine to power its three new Airbus Industries A320 aircraft. International Lease said it is negotiating to buy up to 27 more A320s. Initial aircraft deliveries are planned for 1991, the company said.
