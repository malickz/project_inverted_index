BRISTOL-MYERS BMY REVIEWING SCIMED MERGER

SciMed Life Systems Inc SMLS said Bristol-Myers Co is analyzing the pending lawsuit brought against SciMed by Advanced Cardiovascular Systems Inc to determine whether to consummate its previously announced plans to merge with SciMed. The company said its was served the suit in Minneapolis on March 31, the day after it announced its definitive merger agreement with Bristol-Myers. SciMed said the suit, which alleges that SciMed infringed on Advanced Cardiovascular patents, is without merit.
