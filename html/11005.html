VIDEO DISPLAY VIDE IN INDIAN JOINT VENTURE

Video Display Corp said it signed agreements with Glowtronics Ltd to jointly build a manufacturing plant in the state of Karnatake, India. Video Display said the plant, in which it will own a 40 pct equity interest, will make cathodes, filament heatrers and electron gun assembles for television sets.
