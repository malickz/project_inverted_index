AMERICAN STORES ASC SEES LOWER YEAR NET

American Stores Co said it expects to report earnings per share of 3.70 to 3.85 dlrs per share on sales of slightly over 14 billion dlrs for the year ended January 31. The supermarket chain earned 4.11 dlrs per share on sales of 13.89 billion dlrs last year. The company did not elaborate.
