CABLEVISION SYSTEMS CVCC BUYS STATIONS

Cablevision Systems Corp said it bought cable television systems in six Dutchess County, N.Y., communities from Dutchess County Cablevision Associates Ltd. The company said the acquisition of the new systems will add to its presence in New York. It said it will add 4,400 subsribers to its 568,000 subscriber base.
