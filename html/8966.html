TECHNIMED CORP SAID COMPANY CANCELLED CONTRACT

Technimed Corp said Garid Inc of Eden Praries, Minn., has cancelled its requirements contract wih Technimed for the supply of a dry chemistry device for the Med-Pen system. Technimed has commenced arbitration proceeding against Garid to recover damages associated with the termination of contract. Technimed said further action maybe required to protect its proprietary technology.
