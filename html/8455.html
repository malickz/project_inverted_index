HERLEY MICROWAVE SYSTEMS HRLY GETS CONTRACTS

Herley Microwave Systems Inc said it received 8,300,000 dlrs in contracts from the Westinghouse Electric Corp WX to manufacture super components for the F-16 fighter jet programs. The awards are scheduled for delivery in 1987, 1988 and early 1989, the company said.
