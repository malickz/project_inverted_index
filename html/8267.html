SINGER SMF GETS EIGHT MLN DLR ARMY CONTRACT

Singer Co said its Dalmo Victor Division has received an eight mln dlr contract for full-scale engineering development of a special version of the APR-39A radar threat warning system. It said the contract calls for delivery of 20 development units and includes an option for an initial production quantity of 300 units.
