U.S. SUGAR USUG.O PRESIDENT TO RETIRE

John Boy, president and chief executive officer of the U.S. Sugar Corp, said he will retire effective June 30, 1987. He will be replaced by J. Nelson Fairbanks, currently senior vice president, the company said. Boy will be the vice chairman of the board effective July One, 1987.
