GENEX GNEX NAMES NEW PRESIDENT AND CHIEF EXEC

Genex Corp said Gary Frashier was elected its president and chief executive officer, effective April 1. The company said he replaces Leslie Glick. Frashier is currently chairman and chief executive officer of Continental Water Systems Corp, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Olin Corp OLN.
