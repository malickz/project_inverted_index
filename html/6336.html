18-MAR-1987 00:20:32.30

ONE DEAD AS PHILIPPINE ARMY SCHOOL BOMBED

A bomb blast at the Philippine Military Academy killed at least one person and wounded about 40 during rehearsals for a weekend visit by President Corazon Aquino, military and hospital officials said. Reporters at the scene told s the death toll was likely to rise, but there was no official confirmation. Military sources at first suggested the bomb might have been planted by communist rebels, but no group immediately claimed responsibility.
