VANGUARD TECHNOLOGIES VTI ACQUIRES COMPANY

Vanguard Technologies International Inc said it acquired 100 pct of the stock of Diversified Data Corp. Diversified, and its wholly-owned subsidiary, TECHPRO Corp, have current revenues from operations of approximately four mln dlrs, Vanguard said. The acquisition was for cash, but the amount was not disclosed.
