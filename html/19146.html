INTEGRATED RESOURCES IRE UNIT OFFERS DEBT

Investment banker Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc said it has privately placed 180 mln dlrs of multi-class real estate mortgage investment conduits, or REMIC's, of Integrated Resources Inc's Capitol Life Insurance Co subsidiary. The company said the REMIC's were placed in two series of about 300 seasoned multifamily and commercial loans each.
