BELL CANADA BCE BUYS SHARES ISSUED BY UNIT

Bell Canada Enterprises Inc said it acquired 150 mln dlrs of common shares issued by its wholly owned telephone unit, Bell Canada. Bell said the transaction is expected to be completed on April 15, 1987, and proceeds to the telephone business will be used in part for capital expenditures and to provide additional working funds.
