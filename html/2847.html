LANDMARK SAVINGS LSA COMPLETES OFFICE SALE

Landmark Savings Association said it completed the sale of its Whitehall, Pa., office, including deposits of about 31 mln dlrs, to Parkvale Savings Association. Landmark said it realized a gain of about 1.1 mln dlrs on the sale. The price was not disclosed.
