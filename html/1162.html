CANONIE ENVIRONMENTAL CANO GETS CONTRACT

Canonie Environmental Services Corp said it signed a contract to design and build a ground water cleanup system for Purex Industries Inc to be installed at a Mitchel Field Transit Authority site in Nassau County, N.Y. Canonie said the contract for work, which will complete a project begun earlier at the site, is worth "several million dlrs."
