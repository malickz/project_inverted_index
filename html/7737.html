COGECO BUYS FM STATION, PLANS SHARE ISSUE

Cogeco Inc said it agreed to acquire 100 pct of Quebec City radio station CJMF-FM and will issue subordinated voting shares of the company to cover a portion of the purchase price. It said the purchase price and other terms of the transaction have not been disclosed. The transaction is subject to approval of the Canadian Radio-Television and Telecommunications Commission.
