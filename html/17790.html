RALSTON PURINA RAL FORMS BRANDED FOODS GROUP

Ralston Purina Co said it formed a new branded foods group to align all of its warehouse-delivered human foods. W. Patrick McGinnis, formerly executive vice president of the company's grocery products division, was named president of the new group and will report to W.P. Stiritz, Ralston's chairman.
