NEW JERSEY INVESTOR BUYS 5.2 PCT OF KINARK KIN

A New Jersey investor, Joseph Falkenstein, told the Securities and Exchange Commission he holds 5.2 pct of the shares of Kinark Corp. He said he has no plans to change the company and that he bought the shares because he believed the stock was undervalued and would rise in the next six months.
