JEFFERIES MAKES MARKET GENCORP, AMERICAN EXPRESS

Jefferies and Co said this morning that it is making a market in Gencorp GY. The bid is 106 and the ask is 111. Jefferies also said it is making a market in American Express AXP. Jeffries opened the stock and the bid is 79-1/2 and the ask is 81.
