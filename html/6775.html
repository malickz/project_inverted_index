COCA-COLA BOTTLING COKE FILES TO OFFER NOTES

Coca-Cola Bottling Group Inc said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 125 mln dlrs of subordinated notes due 1997 through Salomon Brothers Inc. Proceeds will be used to repay outstanding debt incurred to finance recent acquisitions, the soft drink bottler said.
