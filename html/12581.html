REAGAN TO LOBBY SENATORS ON HIGHWAY BILL

President Reagan is going to the U.S. Capitol to personally urge senators to support his veto of an 88 billion dlr highway and mass transit funding bill, Republican leader Robert Dole of Kansas said. Reagan will meet with senators at 1115 est/1615 gmt Dole told the senate.
