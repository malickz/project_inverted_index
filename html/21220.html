GRANGES GXL FILES FOR STOCK OFFERING

Granges Exploration LTD, a mining company, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed publioffering of 2,500,000 shares of its common. PaineWebber Inc and Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc will be co-managers of the underwriting group. Granges said it intends to use proceeds for general corporate purposes.
