CIS CISIF.O AGREES TO SECOND EXTENSION

CIS Technologies Inc said that it and the Swiss Reinsurance Co of Zurich, Switzerland agreed to a second extension of two dates for the final part of their share purchase agreement. It said the June one election date has been extended to June 15 and the June 30 closing date has been changed to July 31.
