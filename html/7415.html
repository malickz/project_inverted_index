BUDGET RENT A CAR TO FILE INITIAL OFFERING

Budget Rent a Car Corp said it intends to file a registration for an initial public offering of common shares. It said the offering would solely be comprised of new stock issued by the company. Budget was acquired on September 30, 1986 from Transamerica Corp TA by an investor group led by Gibbons, Green van Amerongen and members of Budget's senior management.
