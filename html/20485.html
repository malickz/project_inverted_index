ERICSSON SELLS OFFICE MACHINE DIVISION

Telefon AB L M Ericsson ERIC.ST said it would sell its office machinery unit, with a turnover of two billion crowns, to Norway's Norska Design Funktion A/S. Ericsson Information Systems, of which the unit is a part, said in a statement a decision would be reached in November about when the Norwegian firm would take over the operation. No price was given for the deal. EIS managing director Stig Larsson said the deal would allow EIS to concentrate on voice and data communication products.
