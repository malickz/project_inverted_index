EC SUGAR IMPORT LEVIES

The following import levies for white and raw sugar are effective tomorrow, in European currency units (ecu) per 100 kilos. White sugar, denatured and non-denatured 49.70 versus 50.05 previously. Raw sugar, denatured and non-denatured 41.35 versus 41.35 previously. The rate for raws is based on 92 pct yield.
