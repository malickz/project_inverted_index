GUNZE SANGYO ISSUING 25 MLN SWISS FRANC NOTES

Gunze Sangyo Inc of Tokyo is issuing 25 mln Swiss francs of five year convertible notes with a 1-1/2 pct coupon, lead manager Swiss Volksbank said. The conversion price has been set at 463 yen per share, compared with today's 450 yen close. Payment is due March 30.
