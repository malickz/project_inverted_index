AETNA AET TO SELL CANADIAN OPERATIONS

Aetna Life and Casualty Co said its Aetna Life and Casualty of Canada Ltd subsidiary has agreed in prnciple to sell its casualty-property subsidiary, Aetna Casualty Co of Canada to Laurentian Group of Montreal for undisclosed terms. The company said the agreement is subject to Canadian government approval.
