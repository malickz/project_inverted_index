AMOSKEAG CO NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

Amoskeag Co said Joseph B. Ely II, became chairman, in addition to his duties as chief executive officer, succeeding Albert B. Hunt, who served in that position since 1972. It also said James M. Fitzgibbons, currently executive vice president and chief operating officer, will beomce president, a position Ely also held.
