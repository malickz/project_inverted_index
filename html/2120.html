NEECO NEEC SHARE OFFERING UNDER WAY

Moseley Holdings Corp MOSE said an offering of 1,700,000 common shares of NEECO Inc is under way at 18.25 dlrs each. The company is selling 1,200,000 of the shares and shareholders the rest. The shareholders have granted underwriters an overallotment option to buy up to 255,000 more shares.
