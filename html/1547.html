WEST GERMAN BANK ISSUES LUXEMBOURG FRANC BOND

Landesbank Rheinland-Pfalz International SA is issuing a 300 mln Luxembourg franc private placement bond with a 7-1/4 pct coupon at par, lead manager Caisse d'Epargne de l'Etat du Grand-Duche de Luxembourg said. The five-year, non-callable bullet bond is for payment on March 24 and coupon payments are annually on March 25.
