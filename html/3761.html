CREDITHRIFT FINANCIAL TO REDEEM NOTES

(Credithrift Financial Corp) said its board authorized the redemption of the company's 8.20 pct Senior Notes due Dec 15, 1987. It said the notes are to be redeemed April 20 at a price equal to 100 pct of the principal amount of the notes plus accrued interest.
