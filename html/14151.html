ALIAS RESEARCH IN CAPITAL JOINT VENTURE

Alias Research Inc said it entered into a four mln dlr joint venture capital investment with several firms led by TA Associates of Boston. Joining TA Associates are Greylock Management Corp, and Crownx of Toronto, Alias said. Alias, which develops 3D computer graphics software, said the investment capital will be used to support ongoing research and development and to extend worldwide marketing initiatives.
