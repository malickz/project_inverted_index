VARITY VAT UNIT HAS NEW INDUSTRIAL DIESELS

Varity Corp said Perkins Engines, Varity's British-based diesel engine subsidiary, introduced three families of new industrial diesels, ranging from 37 to 400 horsepower. The new models include four and six cylinder units, two to 12 liters in displacement, Varity said.
