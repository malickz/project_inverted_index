CHRYSLER C VALUES AMC AMO AT 757 MLN DLRS

Chrysler Corp said it valued its proposed buyout of American Motors Corp at 757 mln dlrs, not counting the effect of a contingent payment that could reach 350 mln dlrs based on AMC's future profits. A Chrysler spokesman told s that the letter of intent signed with Renault, AMC's controlling shareholder with a 46.2 pct direct stake, includes a provision for Renault to be paid 35 mln dlrs in cash for AMC's finance subsidiary and 200 mln dlrs in the form of an eight pct note. He said he did not know the maturity of the note.
