PENNZOIL SAYS SETTLEMENT MUST EXCEED 2 BLN DLRS

Pennzoil Co PZL Chairman J. Hugh Liedtke told a meeting of securities analysts that any settlement of the ongoing litigation would have to be above two billion dlrs. "If anyone thinks this could be settled in the two billion dlrs range, they're not being realistic," he said.
