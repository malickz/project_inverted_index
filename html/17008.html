AHED AHM.TO MAY ISSUE ONE MLN SHARES

Ahed Corp said it began discussions with an investment dealer about issuing an additional one mln treasury shares in the near future. Ahed also said it believes recent increases in its stock price could be attributable to a published report describing the activities of the company. Ahed said no other events are known to have taken place which would cause these recent price increases.
