CHEROKEE GROUP CHKE FILES TO OFFER SHARES

Cherokee Group said it has filed for an offering of 2,500,000 common shares, including 700,000 to be sold by shareholders, through underwriters First Boston Inc FBC and American Express Co AXP unit Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc. It said it will use its proceeds to repay bank debt, for capital spending and for general corporate purposes including possible acquisitions.
