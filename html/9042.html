VR BUSINESS BROKERS EXPANDS OPERATIONS

VR Business Brokers said it sold a master franchise license to one of the largest independent groups of management consultants in the Caribbean. It said that under the master license the group will operate under the name VR Caribbean Inc and will cover countries of the Caribbean basin and Central America and Dade County, Fla. Terms of the sale were not disclosed.
