PARIBAS SHARES TO BE QUOTED ON MAIN PARIS MARKET

Shares in financial group Cie Financiere de Paribas PARI.PA, privatised in January, will begin trading on the Paris Bourse monthly settlement market on April 23, France's Association of Stockbrokers said. The shares are currently traded on the immediate settlement market.
