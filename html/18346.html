FORD F HAILS VOLKER, GREENSPAN

Ford Motor Co praised President Reagan's nomination of Alan Greenspan as Federal Reserve Board chairman, saying the conservative economist has a "sound record to fill the void of (Paul) Volker's departure." The number two U.S. auto maker said in a statement that Volker "took office...at the beginning of a critical time for the auto industry" and had had "a very positive impact on the U.S. economy."
