INTERFINCO SETS 50 MLN SFR FIVE-YEAR CONVERTIBLE

Interfinco S.A. Of Luxembourg is launching a 50 mln Swiss franc five-year convertible bond with an indicated coupon of 2-1/2 pct, lead manager S.G. Warburg Soditic SA said. The issue, guaranteed by Cofide-Compagnia Finanziaria de Benedetti SpA of Turin, Italy, is convertible into shares of CIR SpA - Compagnie Industriali Riunite at an indicated premium of five pct.
