ERICSSON WINS ARKANSAS STATE CONTRACT

Sweden's Telefon AB L.M. Ericsson ERIC ST said one of its units had won a contract worth 8.7 mln dlrs from the U.S. State of Arkansas to provide a communications network for three state buildings. The voice/data communications system will connect 8,000 users via the Ericsson MD 110 subscriber exchange, the company said.
