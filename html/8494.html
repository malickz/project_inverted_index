ANCHOR SAVINGS BANK MEMBERS APPROVE CONVERSION

Anchor Savings Bank board of trustees said a requisite majority of depositors and borrowers, by a vote of 37,531,576 to 714,490, approved Anchor's plan to convert from a federally chartered mutual savings bank to a federally chartered stock savings bank. Anchor had assets of 7.3 billion dlrs as of June 30, 1986, it said.
