KAPOK CORP KPK CURES TECHNICAL DEFAULTS

Kapok Corp said it has cured technical defaults on laons from Southeast Banking Corp STB and Murray Steinfeld and has negotiated extensions of both loans. The company also said it has arranged a one mln dlr loan secured by its Fort Lauderdale, Fla., property from Republic Bank for Savings of Jackson, Miss., and has sold its Peter Pan Restaurant in Urbana, Md., for 1,100,000 dlrs.
