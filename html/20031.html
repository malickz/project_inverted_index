BANGLADESH TENDERS FOR TWO MLN BARRELS PETROLEUM

Bangladesh Petroleum Corp said it floated an international tender for imports of two mln barrels of Jet Kero, Superior Kero and High Speed Diesel for shipment during January-June 1988. It said the offer for the petroleum products would be open until 0600 gmt on November 19.
