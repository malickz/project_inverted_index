24-MAR-1987 11:00:38.49

AMER SIGNS 50 MLN DOLLAR MULTICURRENCY LOAN

Finland's business group Amer-Yhtyma Oy YHTY.HE said it has signed a 50 mln dlr, six-year syndicated multicurrency loan with a group of 15 international and four Finnish banks led by Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd. Amer said in a statement the loan would be used mainly to convert its overseas currency loans and expand business, but gave no further details.
