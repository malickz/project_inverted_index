RAYTHEON RTN RECEIVES 37.5 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Raytheon Co said it was awarded a 37.5 mln Navy contract to provide engineering and technical services for production of the standard Missle II program. This award provides for engineering support to the 230-missle second source production contract currently under way at Raytheon's missile systems divsion facility, the company said.
