FRUIT OF THE LOOM INC INITIAL OFFERING STARTS

Fruit of the Loom Inc said an initial public offering of 27 mln Class A common shares is underway at nine dlrs per share through underwriters led by Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc, Merrill Lynch and Co Inc MER, E.F. Hutton Group Inc EFH and Sears, Roebuck and Co Inc's S Dean Witter Reynolds Inc unit.
