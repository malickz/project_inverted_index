DATAPRODUCTS DPC GETS SIEMENS CONTRACT

Dataproducts Corp said it received a multi-year contract, valued at more than 30 mln dlrs, to supply band printers to Siemens AG of West Germany. Siemens will use the printers for office applications and for medium to large mainframes, Dataproducts said.
