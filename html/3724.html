PENTAIR PNTA FILES TO OFFER PREFERRED

Pentair Inc said it has filed for an offering of two mln shares of cumulative convertible preferred stock at an expected price of 25 dlrs per share through underwriters led by Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc and Piper, Jaffray and Hopwood Inc. It said proceeds would be used to repay bank debt incurred in the acquisition of McNeil Corp last August.
