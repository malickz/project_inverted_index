TAIWAN BUYS 54,000 TONNES OF U.S. SOYBEANS

The joint committee of Taiwan soybean importers awarded a contract to Cigra Inc of Chicago to supply a 54,000 tonne cargo of U.S. Soybeans, a committee spokesman told s. The cargo, priced at 213.87 U.S. Dlrs per tonne c and f Taiwan, is for delivery between April 20 and May 5.
