GENERAL NUTRITION GNC SHARES TO BE SOLD

General Nutrition Inc said it has filed for a secondary offering of eight mln common shares. It said all the shares are being sold by the estate of founder David B. Shakarian. Lead underwriter will be Kidder, Peabody and Co Inc. General Nutrition has about 33.0 mln shares outstanding.
