INTERNATIONAL FINE FOODS MAKES ACQUISITION

International Fine Foods Inc said it has acquired 2001 Distributors Inc of West Babylon, N.Y., for 875,000 common shares, plus contingent shares based on future earnings. 2001 distributes fresh-squeezed juices and had a loss on sales of about one mln dlrs in 1986, its first year of operation.
