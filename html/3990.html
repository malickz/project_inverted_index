BROAD ACQUIRES VOGT AND CONANT UNIT

Broad Corp said it acquired the construction activities of Vogt and Conant Co of Cleveland. The combined companies, to be called Broad, Vogt and Conant INc, will be the largest structural steel erection company in the U.S. Combined sales of the two operations were more than 40 mln dlrs in 1986.
