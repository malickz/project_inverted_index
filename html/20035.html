FABER OPEN FOR OFFERS ON MORGAN GRENFELL STAKE

Willis Faber Plc WIFL.L chairman and chief executive David Palmer said the company would consider any bid for its 20.8 pct shareholding in Morgan Grenfell Group Plc MGFL.L but had not yet received any offers. "We will entertain any approaches," he told s in reply to questions, following U.K. Press speculation. In an earlier statement, Faber said that if an offer were to be received for its stake in the merchant banking group, "it would be considered on its merits."
