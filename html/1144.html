ANITEC IMAGE ANTC FILES FOR DEBT OFFERING

Anitec Image Technology Inc said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed offering of 60 mln dlrs in convertible subordinated debentures. Anitec said the proceeds will be used for acquisitions, joint ventures and other investments. Anitec said the offering is being underwritten by Smith Barney, Harris Upham and Co, Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette Securities, Salomon Inc SB, and Mabon Nugent and Co.
