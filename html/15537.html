BIOTHERAPEUTICS BITX REPORTS DRUG FINDINGS

Biotherapeutics Inc said its cooperative findings on interleukin-2, the anti-cancer drug, were published today in the "New England Journal of Medicine". Biotherapeutics, along with the Biological Therapy Institute and the National Cancer Institute, said the publication focused on a reduction in toxicity through the use of a constant infusion mode of intravenous administration protocol.
