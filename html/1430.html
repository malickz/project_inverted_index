AUSTIN ROVER GROUP REPORTS SALES ON STERLINGS

Great Britain's Austin Rover Group reported the sale of 1,043 Sterling automobiles during the second half of February, the first month in which the European luxury car was on sale. The company said the Sterling 825S sells for 19,000 dlrs and the Sterling 825SL sells for 23,900 dlrs, and are aimed at the European luxury sedan market. The Sterling was designed in a joint venture with Honda Motor Corp HMC. The Rover Group was the former British Leland PLC company, according to the company.
