STOLTENBERG ASSUMES COOPERATION WILL CONTINUE

The West German government assumes that the commitment to international monetary cooperation which was renewed in Washington last month will continue, a Finance Ministry spokesman quoted Finance Minister Gerhard Stoltenberg as saying. Stoltenberg's statement was a reaction to criticism of rises in West German interest rates voiced by U.S. Treasury Secretary James Baker over the weekend.
