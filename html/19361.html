BRAINTREE SAVINGS BTSB.O NAMES NEW CEO

Braintree Savings Bank said Winthrop Sargent IV has been named president and chief executive officer, succeeding Lindsay L. Tait, who remains chairman. It said Sargent, who was senior vice president, has also been named to the board. It said Tait intends to retire in about four years.
