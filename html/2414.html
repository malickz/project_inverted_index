MANOR CARE INC MNR CHAIRMAN RESIGNS

Manor Care Inc said its chairman and chief executive officer, Stewart Bainum Sr., has resigned his post. The company said he is being replaced by his son, Stewart Bainum Jr., who has served as vice chairman the past five years. Bainum Sr. was the founder of Manor Care, a nursing home and hotel operator.
