MOORE MCCORMACK MMC TO SELL GLOBE UNIT

Moore McCormack Resources Inc said it agreed in principle to sell its Globe Metallurgical Inc unit to Cyprus Minerals Co CYPM for undisclosed terms. The sale, expected to close by mid-April, is subject to certain conditions, including board approval at both companies. Globe produces silicon metals and magnesium ferrosilicon. The products are used in the chemical, aluminum, iron, rubber and semiconductor industries.
