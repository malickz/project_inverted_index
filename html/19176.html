GTE GTE UNIT GETS CONTRACT FOR PRODUCTS

GTE Corp said its Fiber Optic Products division was awarded a 250,000 dlr minimum contract by NYNEX Corp's NYN Enterprise Co. The bulk of the agreement applies to GTE's elastomeric glass tube splice products, the company said, as well as other fiber optic components, including GTE's recently-introduced cleaving tool and installation kit.
