OMAHA CATTLE SEEN STEADY/UP 0.50 DLR - PRIVATE

Cattle prices are expected to range from steady to 0.50 dlr higher on estimated receipts of 2,700 head, private sources said. Steer top seen at 68.00 and Heifers at 66.50 dlrs. Sources said death losses that were reported in part of the high plains areas were causing concern in the industry.
