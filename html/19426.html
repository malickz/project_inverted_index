ARCHER-DANIELS ADM DENIES RUMOR

Archer-Daniels-Midland Co denied a report that it is interested in acquiring International Minerals and Chemical Corp (IGL). "We have no interest in International Minerals or any of its divisions," a spokesman told s. "We've had no conversations with them." USA Today reported that Archer-Daniels-Midland might be seeking a hostile takeover of International Minerals.
