SPACE MICROWAVE SMLI SETS SECURITIES OFFERING

Space Microwave Laboratories Inc said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission a secondary offering of 2,200 units of its securities at 1,000 dlrs per unit. Each unit consists of shares of common stock, common stock purchase warrants and 1,000-dlr principal amount of convertible subordinated debentures. RLR Securities Group Inc will underwrite the offering.
