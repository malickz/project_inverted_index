SHELDAHL SHEL IN AGREEMENT WITH SUMITOMO

Sheldahl Inc said it signed the previously announced agreements with Sumitomo Bakelite Co Ltd, Tokyo, Japan, providing for a joint participation program for manufacturing flexible circuits and circuitry components. Under terms of the agreement, each company granted the other a license to use flexible circuit and related technology. As part of the transaction, Sumitomo Bakelite has purchased 10 pct, or 325,000 shares of Sheldahl common stock. In addition, Sheldahl will be a minority holder in Sumitomo's next flexible circuit manufacturing operation in Japan.
