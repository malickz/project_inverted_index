MEAD MEA SELLS DEBENTURES AT NINE PCT

A 150 mln dlr issue of Mead Corp 30-year debentures was given a nine pct coupon and priced at par, Smith Barney, Harris Upham and Co Inc said as lead manager. The securities, which are not callable for 10 years, carry a rating of A-3 from Moody's Investors Service Inc and one of BBB-plus from Standard and Poor's Corp. Mead said it will use the proceeds to retire outstanding short-term debt, a portion of which was incurred to finance part of recent acquisitions.
