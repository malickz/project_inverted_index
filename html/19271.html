N-D RESOURCES NUDY.O AGREES TO ACQUIRE ATOKA

N-D Resources Inc said it agreed in principle to issue an undetermined number of shares to Recovery Resources Corp, Bahrain, in order to acquire Recovery's Atoka Gas Gathering Systems Inc. Atoka currently operates a 20-mile natural gas pipeline of 46 miles in length in southeastern Oklahoma.
