MID-STATE MSSL, FIRST FEDERAL IN DEAL

Mid-State Federal Savings and Loan Association said it and First Federal Savings and Loan Association of Brooksville FFBV reached a definitive merger agreement. As previously announced, Brooksville shareholders will get cash and stock in exchange for their shares. The transaction is expected to be completed during the summer 1987.
