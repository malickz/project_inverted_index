ELSINORE ELS MAKES DEPOSIT TO COVER BONDS

Elsinore Corp said it deposited five mln dlrs to cover accrued and unpaid interest through June 30 of the 15-1/2 pct senior mortgage bonds of 1999 of its unit Elsinore Finance Corp. Elsinore has guaranteed the payment of its unit's bonds. The parent deposited the five mln dlrs with Elsinore Finance's trustee, Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co, it said.
