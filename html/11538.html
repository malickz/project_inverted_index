E-SYSTEMS ESY RECEIVES ORDER FROM BOEING BA

E-Systems Inc said it received during the first quarter 5.7 mln dlrs of follow-on orders from Boeing Co's Boeing Commercial Airplane Co unit. E-Systems said that under the new orders it will produce several different hydraulic and electrohydraulic components that are used with Boeing 737, 747, 757 and 767 aircraft.
