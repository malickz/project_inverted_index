SWISSAIR REVENUES FALL FOUR PCT IN APRIL

Swissair SWSZ.Z said its revenues were down four pct in April compared with a year earlier, but costs before depreciation were cut by six pct. Traffic rose seven pct in April compared with a year earlier and the overall load factor increased one percentage point to 61 pct. Passenger load factor rose to 60 pct from 59 pct.
