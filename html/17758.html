CCC INTEREST HIGHER IN JUNE, USDA SAYS

Interest rates on commodity loans disbursed by the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) this month will carry a 6-7/8 pct interest rate, the U.S. Agriculture Department said. That is up from the May rate of 6-1/4 pct and reflects the interest rate charged the CCC by the U.S. Treasury in June, USDA noted.
