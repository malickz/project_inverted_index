VERNITRON VRN SETS NEW RECORD DATE

Vernitron Corp said it has set a new record date of July 10 for shareholders entitled to vote on the proposed merger of Vernitron with SB Holding Corp. The original record date was May 26. Vernitron said that it currently expects the special shareholder meeting concerning the merger will be held in August. Following its tender offer in November 1986, SB Holding holds 55.2 pct of Vernitron, a maker of electromechanical components and related products and services.
