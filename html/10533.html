GENCORP GY CHANGES SITE OF ANNUAL MEETING

GenCorp Inc said it changed the location for its March 31 annual meeting to the Quaker Sqaure Hilton, Akron. The company said it changed the location of the meeting because of an expected increase in attendance. A partnership of AFG Industries Inc AFG and Wagner and Brown have made a 100 dlr a share tender offer for GenCorp.
