BENIHANA BNHN TO BUILD RESTAURANT IN FLORIDA

Benihana National Corp said it signed a letter of intent with Tishman Realty and Construction Co Inc to build a teppanyaki-style restaurant at the Hilton Hotel at Walt Disney World Village in Lake Buena Vista, Fal. Benihana National said it currently owns and operates 16 teppanyaki-style restaurants under license from its parent, Benihana of Tokyo Inc.
