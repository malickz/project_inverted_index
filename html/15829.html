ROYAL DUTCH RD UNIT TO CUT HEATING OIL PRICE

Royal Dutch/Shell's Scallop Petroleum Co subsidiary said it will reduce the price it charges contract barge customers in New York harbor for heating oil by 2.5 cts a gallon, effective tomorrow. The company said the price cut will bring the contract barge price for heating oil to 51 cts a gallon.
